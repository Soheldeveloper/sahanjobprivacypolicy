Privacy Policy

Welcome to SahanJob. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how SahanJob ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our job marketplace platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us to facilitate the recruitment process:

  • Job Seekers: Full name, email address, phone number, physical address, professional experience, education, skills, and CV/resume files.
  • Employers: Company name, business registration details, contact person information, and job descriptions.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and usage patterns collected via cookies to improve site performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable candidates to build and store CVs and apply for jobs.
  • To allow employers to review applications and manage the recruitment pipeline.
  • To provide notifications regarding job alerts or application status.
  • To improve our platform’s features and security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • With Employers: When a job seeker applies for a position, their profile and CV are shared with that specific employer.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party vendors for cloud hosting or email notifications. These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by Somali law or the National Communications Authority (NCA).

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest and in transit (SSL/TLS).
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access sensitive database information.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security reviews to identify vulnerabilities.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update or correct your profile information at any time.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your account and all associated CVs.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can opt-out of marketing communications.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If a job seeker remains inactive for 2 years, we may anonymize or delete the data.
